About Our Project

This project addresses Myanmar's growing digital divide by training university instructors from across the country in essential ICT domains such as IT fundamentals, networking (CCNA), and cybersecurity operations.

These capacity-building efforts help institutions enhance their technical teaching resources, enable the delivery of certified ICT courses, and pave the way for more universities to join the Cisco Networking Academy framework with a strong focus on sustainable impact, gender inclusion, and national reach.

Our Mission

To build national ICT capacity by training university instructors in key areas of networking and cybersecurity to bridge Myanmar's digital divide and strengthen digital education across the nation.

Gender Inclusion

Promoting Gender Equality in ICT

Our project demonstrates exceptional gender inclusion with 86% female participation among trained instructors. This achievement highlights Myanmar's commitment to empowering women in technology education and leadership roles.

While gender participation was remarkably high, we continue to support female instructors in advancing to leadership and decision-making positions within their institutions.
